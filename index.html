BoxPvP

BoxPvP is a popular game mode on Minecraft servers in which players fight each other in an arena (usually a “box” – an enclosed area), using equipment obtained through progression. The main goal is to become as strong as possible, win PvP (player vs player) battles, collect resources and money, and climb to the top of the server. Usually, there are special enchantment systems, crates, missions, and upgrades that make the gameplay more dynamic and competitive..

Survival

Survival is one of the most classic game modes in Minecraft, where players must survive in an open world by gathering resources, building shelters, and exploring. You start from scratch and create your own progress: mining, farming, crafting, and developing your base. On servers, survival mode is often enhanced with economy systems, land defense, missions, and events, providing a more interactive and competitive experience with other players.

▓
Factions

Factions is a Minecraft game mode focused on strategy, alliances, and PvP between groups of players. Each player can create or join a faction, with the goal of building a base, gathering resources, and dominating the server. Players can form alliances or wage war with other factions, attacking bases and stealing resources. It is a competitive mode where teamwork, tactics, and territory protection are all important.

▒
OneBlock

OneBlock is a Minecraft game mode where you start with a single block that, when broken, regenerates and provides you with different resources in stages. Basically, from that single block you build your entire world: platforms, base, equipment and progress. As you continue to break the block, you progress through phases (such as forest, nether, end), each with increasingly advanced resources and challenges. It is a progressive survival mode, based on luck and continuous development.

▒
BedWars

BedWars is a PvP game mode in Minecraft where teams defend their own bed and try to destroy the beds of their opponents. As long as your bed is intact, you can respawn after you die, but if it is destroyed, you can no longer respawn. Players collect resources from islands (gold, iron, diamonds) to buy weapons, armor, blocks, and upgrades. The ultimate goal is to eliminate all other teams and be the last one standing.
